Details about user verification processes.

Charpair Ensures That Vehicle Details Shared On The Platform Are Reviewed For Accuracy And Transparency. Verified Vehicle Information Helps Riders Choose Trusted Cars And Travel With More Confidence. Q: Why Is Vehicle Verification Important? Verifying Your Vehicle Assures Users That Your Vehicle Is Safe, Insured, And Suitable For Ride-Sharing. It Helps Avoid Fake Or Outdated Vehicle Details. This Builds Trust And Can Increase Your Chance Of Getting More Lifty Bookings. Q: What Documents Do I Need For Verification?
  • Vehicle Model (E.G. Hyundai i10)
  • Registration Number
  • Category (Hatchback/Sedan/SUV)
  • Insurance Validity Or Expiry Date
  • Fare Per KM
  • Number Of Seats Available
  • Clear Front & Back Images Of The Vehicle
  • RC Image (Registration Certificate)
For Bikes, Similar Details Apply (Model, Insurance, RC, Vehicle Images, Etc.). Q: How Do I Upload These Documents?
  1. Go To Your Profile > My Vehicle Section.
  2. Enter Details And Upload Required Documents/Photos.
  3. Submit For Review.
Q: How Long Does Vehicle Verification Take? If All Details Are Correct And Clear, It Usually Takes 24-48 Hours. Q: What If My Verification Is Rejected? You Will Receive A Message With The Reason (E.G. Blurred Image, Expired Insurance). You Can Resubmit Corrected Details For Another Review.
