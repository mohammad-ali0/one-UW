Frequently Asked Question

YOUR QUESTIONS ANSWERED

Find Answers To The Most Common Questions About CharPair, Verification, Payments, Safety, And More.

🛡️✔️ User Profile Verification Before booking or offering a ride on CharPair, every user must complete a profile verification process. We review essential information to ensure users are genuine, accountable, and safe to interact with. This helps build a trusted carpooling community. Q: Why should I verify my profile? Profile verification builds trust within the CharPair platform. A verified badge confirms that you are a real user, not a fake or spam account. This increases your chances of getting ride approvals and ensures safer connections between riders and drivers. Q: What is required for profile verification? • A valid Photo ID (NID, Driving License, etc.)
• A clear personal profile picture (no random images)
• Basic personal details (as required in the app) Q: How do I verify my profile? Go to your Profile section.
Tap on “Verify Profile”.
Upload the required documents and photo.
Submit your profile for review. Q: How long does verification take? Profile verification usually takes 12–24 hours, depending on the quality and clarity of your submission. Q: What if my verification is rejected? If your verification is rejected, you will receive a reason (such as unclear photo or invalid ID). You can correct the issue and resubmit your profile anytime.

🚗🛡️ Vehicle Verification Charpair ensures that vehicle details shared on the platform are reviewed for accuracy and transparency. Verified vehicle information helps riders choose trusted cars and travel with more confidence. Q: Why is vehicle verification important? Verifying your vehicle assures users that your vehicle is safe, insured, and suitable for ride-sharing. It helps avoid fake or outdated vehicle details. This builds trust and can increase your chance of getting more Lifty bookings. Q: What documents do I need for verification? Vehicle Model (e.g., Hyundai i10) Registration Number Category (Hatchback/Sedan/SUV) Insurance Validity or expiry date Fare per KM Number of seats available Clear front & back images of the vehicle RC Image (Registration Certificate) For bikes, similar details apply (model, insurance, RC, vehicle images, etc.). Q: How do I upload these documents? Go to your Profile > My Vehicle section. Enter details and upload required documents/photos. Submit for review. Q: How long does vehicle verification take? If all details are correct and clear, it usually takes 24–48 hours. Q: What if my verification is rejected? You will receive a message with the reason (e.g., blurred image, expired insurance). You can resubmit corrected details for another review.

💳🔄 Payments Claim & Recharge Charpair provides a simple and transparent system for payments, recharge, and claim-related queries. We aim to ensure smooth transactions and clear communication for both riders and vendors. Q: How do payments work on Char Pair? Char Pair uses a wallet system for transactions. Liftys must recharge their Char Pair wallet to pay for rides, and Lifters receive payments into their wallet. Both parties transact within the app, ensuring transparency. Q: What is the Char Pair platform fee? We charge a 10% + applicable tax as platform usage fee: For Liftys: Fee is charged when paying for a ride using the Char Pair wallet. For Lifters: Fee is charged when requesting a wallet redemption (withdrawal). Q: How do I recharge my wallet? Navigate to the Payment section Click Recharge in Payment Section Add amount and click recharge Q: How do I withdraw my earnings as a Lifter? Go to Payments. Tap on "Claim." Minimum withdrawal is ₹500. There is no maximum cap; you can withdraw any available balance. The withdrawal process usually takes 2–3 business days. Q: What if my transaction fails? If a payment fails but money is debited from your account, it is usually refunded automatically within a few hours. If not, contact support with transaction details.

⚙️👤 Account & Preferences Manage your Charpair account details, contact information, notification settings, and travel preferences with ease. Keep your profile updated to enjoy a smoother and more personalized carpooling experience. Q: Can I edit my account information after signup? Yes, you can update your name, profile photo, email, and contact number anytime. Just go to your Profile > Edit Profile section and make the necessary changes. Q: What preferences can I set for ride matches? Char Pair allows you to set Ride Matching Preferences to ensure comfort and compatibility with co-riders. These include: Gender preference (e.g., same gender only) Age range of co-riders Rating filter (minimum rating) Verified users only Professional preference (e.g., office goers, students) Setting these preferences helps the system recommend safer and more relevant rides for you. Q: Can I change my preferences later? Absolutely. You can update your preferences at any time before searching for or offering rides. Go to Settings > Ride Preferences in the app.

🚘🤝 Offer Lift & Find Lift Post your available seats or search for rides that match your route in just a few steps. Charpair makes it simple for car owners and riders to connect, share travel costs, and reduce unnecessary traffic. Q: How do I offer a lift to others? Go to the “Offer Lift” section in the app. Enter your start and end location, date & time, and available seats. Set your preferred fare per km (if applicable) or use suggested rate. Confirm vehicle details and post the ride. Once posted, interested users can request to join your ride. You can accept or reject requests based on their profile, verification, and ride preferences. Q: How do I find a lift for my route? Tap on the “Find Lift” section. Enter your pickup and drop locations, date, and time range. Browse the available rides offered by nearby Lifters. View details such as fare, vehicle type, Lifter rating, and available seats. Request to join the ride and wait for confirmation. You’ll receive a notification when the Lifter accepts your request. Q: Can I cancel a ride after offering or booking it? Yes, but cancellations should be done well in advance to avoid inconvenience to others. Frequent last-minute cancellations may affect your rating. Q: How are fares calculated? Fares are usually calculated on a per kilometer basis, set by the Lifter. You’ll see the estimated fare before booking. Char Pair charges a small platform fee (detailed under the Payments section). Q: Can I chat with the co-rider before confirming? Yes. Once a ride request is initiated, there’s a secure in-app chat feature that allows both parties to coordinate and clarify before finalizing the ride. Q: Is it safe to accept ride requests from strangers? Char Pair prioritizes safety. You can check the profile, verification status, rating, and ride history of each user before accepting their request. You also have full control to accept or reject any request.

🚨🛡️ Safety & Emergency (SOS) Charpair prioritizes user safety with verified profiles, transparent ride details, and emergency support features. In case of unexpected situations, users can access SOS assistance and report concerns directly through the platform. Q: What is the SOS feature and when should I use it? The SOS feature is designed for emergency situations such as feeling unsafe, medical issues, or accidents. When triggered, it instantly notifies your emergency contact with your live location and trip details so they can assist or escalate quickly. Q: What happens when I use the SOS button? When you tap SOS: Your emergency contact receives a notification with your real-time location. You get quick options to call your emergency contact or local help. In future updates, SOS may also notify local authorities. Q: How do I set or update my emergency contact? Go to Profile > Emergency Contact > Enter name and phone number. You can change it any time before or after a ride. Q: Will SOS work without the internet? Without the internet, SOS may not send your live location but will try to use your last known location. You should ensure the internet is active for the best results. Q: Who can use the SOS feature? Everyone. The SOS button is available for both Lifter and Lifty during any ride.

🎁🤝 Refer and Earn Invite your friends to join Charpair and grow the trusted carpooling community. When they sign up and start using the platform, you can enjoy referral benefits as per our program terms. Q: What is the Refer and Earn program? The Refer and Earn program allows users to invite their friends and family to join the Char Pair app. When someone signs up using your referral code, both you and your friend receive a reward of Rs.250. Q: How do I refer someone? Open the Char Pair app. Go to the “Refer Friends” section. Copy your unique referral code (e.g., 12345678). Share it with your friends via WhatsApp, SMS, email, or social media. Ask them to enter this code during registration. Q: What does the referee (my friend) get? Your friend (the referee) will receive a welcome bonus of Rs.250 after signing up using your referral code. Q: What do I get as the referrer? You will receive Rs.250 for each successful referral once your friend downloads the app and completes the sign-up process using your referral code. Q: Is there a limit on how many people I can refer? There is usually no limit to how many people you can refer. The more friends you refer, the more you can earn! However, make sure to check the app's terms and conditions for any updates. Q: When will I receive my referral bonus? Referral bonuses are typically credited immediately after your friend completes their registration and first app activity, or within 24–48 hours, depending on the verification process. Q: Where can I see my referral earnings? You can track all your referral activity and earnings in the “Refer Friends” or “Rewards” section of the app. Detailed information may be available under your wallet or earnings history. Q: My friend forgot to use my code. Can I still get the bonus? Unfortunately, referral bonuses are only valid if the code is entered during the sign-up process. Once the account is created, it cannot be retroactively applied. Q: Can I use my referral code on multiple devices/accounts? No. The referral code is intended for new users only. Attempting to misuse it through multiple accounts or devices may result in suspension of your rewards or account. Q: Is the referral bonus withdrawable? No, the referral bonus cannot be withdrawn to a bank account or external wallet. It can only be used within the Char Pair app for eligible services such as paying for your rides, applying discounts on future bookings, or other in-app services as allowed by the platform.

Once these details are submitted, your account setup will be complete and you can start using all features of the app.

🚗📄 Profile & Vehicle Profile Completion Q: What is Vehicle Profile Completion? Vehicle Profile Completion shows how much of your vehicle information has been added to your Charpair account. The more details you provide, the higher your completion percentage. Q: How is my vehicle profile percentage calculated? Your completion score is calculated based on the details you upload:

Vehicle front, back & side images – 30%
Registration Certificate (RC) – 30%
Insurance validity – 10%
Vehicle model – 10%
Vehicle colour – 10%
Company name – 5%
Manufacturing year – 5%

Adding all required details increases your completion score. Q: What do I need to upload to increase my completion percentage? Clear images of your vehicle (front, rear, and side)
Registration Certificate (RC)
Valid insurance details
Vehicle model, colour, company name, and manufacturing year Q: At what percentage will I get a Verified Badge? Once your vehicle profile reaches 80% completion, it becomes eligible for verification review. After approval, a Verified Badge will be added to your profile. Q: Is uploading RC and insurance mandatory? Uploading RC and insurance details is strongly recommended, as they contribute significantly to your completion percentage and verification eligibility. Q: Why are vehicle photos important? Vehicle photos help maintain transparency and allow riders to identify the car before travel. Clear images also improve trust within the community. Q: Can I update my vehicle details later? Yes. You can update or edit your vehicle details anytime from your account settings to improve your completion score. Q: Will 100% completion automatically verify my vehicle? Reaching 80% makes you eligible for verification. Final approval depends on review of submitted details.
