HOW TO GET STARTED

getting started with charpair is simple and quick. the platform is designed to help users connect easily while keeping cost sharing clear, fair, and transparent.

create your account

Sign up on the CharPair app using your mobile number and complete basic verification. This helps build trust and safety within the community.

Offer or Find a Ride

Lifter(Vehicle's Owner) can post their planned routes in advance. Liftee(Passengers) can search for rides that match their travel direction and timing.

Match and Connect

Users connect only after mutual acceptance. Pickup points, drop locations, and cost sharing details are discussed and agreed upon before the journey begins.

Travel Together

Once confirmed, users travel together, share the cost of the journey, and reach their destination comfortably.

WHY CHOOSE CHARPAIR?

THE 4 PROMISES OF CHARPAIR

charpair is built on four core principles that define our commitment to you. every feature, every interaction, and every journey on our platform is designed around these values.

EASY

Simple, intuitive interface that anyone can use. No complicated steps, no unnecessary features. Just straightforward ride sharing and cost splitting that takes minutes to set up and use.

ECONOMIC

Save money on every journey. Share fuel costs, toll expenses, and travel charges with others heading the same way. Your wallet gets relief, and the environment benefits too.

ECO-FRIENDLY

Reduce your carbon footprint. By sharing rides instead of traveling solo, you contribute to fewer vehicles on the road and lower emissions. Travel responsibly while saving money.

COMFORTABLE

Travel with ease and confidence. Private vehicles, verified drivers, and riders you trust. Enjoy comfort without compromising on safety or convenience during your journey.
